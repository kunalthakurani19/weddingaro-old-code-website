About Us

Founded in 2018, Weddingaro.com is a product of TRIFECTA NET PVT LTD, an Indian IT company. Weddingaro.com team is making it easier to find and book venues and vendors across 30+ cities in India. We are a young team that loves to find all kinds of trendy, chic, professional, or unique venues and vendors. We are building a product that showcases the amazing spaces and event professionals we come across and at the same time, we aim to help event organizers save time and effort when looking for their kind of venues and event partners. The ultimate objective is to connect the right people to the right kind of venues and vendors, bridging the gap and delivering value!
